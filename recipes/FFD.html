Fabulous Football Dip


Recipe

This dip can make with 3 ingredients with sausages, cheese and canned tomatoes. And can be served with tortilla chips or crackers

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sausages
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
  • 2 packages cream cheese, softened

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook sausage over a medium heat until browned and cooked through.
  2. Mix tomatoes and cream cheese into the skillet. Stir and continue cooking mixture over a medium heat until cheese has melted.
